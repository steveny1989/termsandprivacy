Privacy Policy

Thank you for choosing to use ThinkBox! We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information, as well as how you can manage it.

1. Account Registration and Login

1.1 Basic Information Collection
When you register or log in, we may collect your phone number, gender, interests, age, and other basic information to provide you with personalized services. You may skip certain optional steps without affecting your access to core features.

1.2 Third-Party Login
If you log in using a third-party account, we will collect your phone number to complete identity verification in compliance with real-name network requirements.

2. Provision of Products and Services

2.1 Personalized Recommendations
To provide our core functions, we may collect your submitted profile information, browsing history, and activity records (such as likes, favorites, shares, etc.). This data helps us recommend relevant content. You can disable personalized recommendations in the settings if you prefer.

2.2 Permission Authorization
To support upload and publishing features, you need to grant storage permissions (photos, media, and other files). Declining this permission will not affect other functions.

3. Security and Risk Control

3.1 Device Information Collection
To ensure the safety of your information and account, we collect device information (such as device model, network address, IP address, etc.) for risk control. Refusing this may affect risk verification and security measures.

4. Information Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties without your consent, unless for the following reasons:

  • To provide the services you requested;
  • To fulfill contractual obligations;
  • To protect the legal rights of ThinkBox or its users;
  • As required by laws or regulations.

5. Information Storage and Protection

5.1 Retention Period
We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless otherwise required by law.

5.2 Security Measures
We adopt industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, use, alteration, damage, or loss.

6. Your Rights

You can log in to your ThinkBox account at any time to view or modify your personal information. If you believe we are violating any laws or agreements, you may contact us for resolution.

7. Account Deletion

If you no longer wish to use ThinkBox services, you can apply to delete your account via: [Me - Settings - Account & Security - Delete Account]. Account deletion will result in permanent removal of all data and content associated with your account.

8. Third-Party Services

When using third-party services on the ThinkBox platform, those third parties may collect and use your information in accordance with their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing their policies carefully.

9. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience and service quality. You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings. Please note that some services may not function properly if cookies are disabled.
